Current updates & development

Redstone Hollows is curently only in it's very initial development. Not only are Chris and I new to game Devb but both of us work fulltime, me as a Barista and Chris as a Software Engineer. Additionally I just recently relocated from the US to the Netherlands and sometime this year Chris intends to move from Colorado to Prague. Despite all of that we intend to re-start our development on it. It would be wonderful if by early next year we have a Kickstarter for our project to help fund us. We intent to release this game to Steam as some point.

Redstone Hollows is going to be a co-op game. Chris has already begun coding with that in mind and the functionality already coming to fruition. So if you were hoping to play with friends we've got you covered!

Where to see more content

Twitch

I used to stream consistently but hopefully soon I will get back to streaming game dev content on Twitch. I mainly stream me drawing on Aseprite while chatting with viewers. It's a wonderful way for me to get live feedback if I ask and to meet other people with similar interests. Many times it is other game developers who join in on the chats. I also stream other people's indie games to see what other small groups/people are capable of. It's a great way to support other people while also gaining inspiration for myself.

Twitter

On Twitter I post little snippets of drawings and updates on content. I also re-tweet art and games I find inspiring. Sometimes I even re-wteet "how to" content - so tutorials that range from pixel art to 3D modeling.

Youtube

Youtube a space that I do more vlog like content. That means it ranges from all aspects of my life. I am also very into traveling and photography so you can see content of a wider range here. Most "game dev" related content is found through Twitch and Twitter for now. I do plan to eventually post some here but more in the future.
